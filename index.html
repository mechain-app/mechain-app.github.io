A sign of the times


What's a person to do?

  • meChain generates strong passwords and remembers them so you don't have to.
  • meChain synchronizes your passwords across devices so a password entered on your phone is available everywhere you need it, and vice-versa.
  • meChain provides simple but powerful controls to customize passwords according to the requirements/limitations of each site you use.
  • meChain remembers each site's previous password
  • meChain is an inexpensive one-time purchase, so there are no recurring monthly fees.
